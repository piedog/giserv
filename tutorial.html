Zoom to alaska - north slope

  1. Click on Zoom tab
  2. Select State/County radio button
  3. Select Alaska from the State drop down list
  4. Select North Slope from the County drop down list
  5. Press the Quick Zoom button

Zoom in using box drag

  1. Make sure the zoom-in navigation button is pressed in
    • Navigation buttons are the 4 square buttons at the top of the panel to the right of the map
  2. Move mouse pointer on the map somewhere over the clump of yellow seismic lines
  3. Press left mouse button down
  4. Drag about 1 inch down and to the right and release mouse button.
  5. This will zoom in to the area defined by the red box.

Enable additional map layers

  1. Click on the Layers tab
  2. Make the following labels visible by checking the boxes in the Visible column
    • Township/Range
    • County Boundary
    • 2D Seismic Lines
    • Streams and Rivers
    • Lakes
    • State Boundary
  3. Press the Redraw Map button

Perform a geographic query

  1. Make sure the identify navigation button is pressed in of the panel to the left of the map)
  2. Now drag a 2 inch box over the map image This will highlight all seismic lines that intersect or are enclosed by the drag box

Examine selected seismic lines

  1. Click on the Results tab This provides a text list of the selected seismic lines
  2. Move mouse pointer over the id numbers in the Gid column in the result list.
  3. The corresponding seismic line on the map is highlighted.
